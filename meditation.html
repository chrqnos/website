Meditation

I personally started meditation consistently around the same time I started working out.
Meditation is a great way for me to better my mental health and
get rid of stress or any fatigue build up.
It also helps greatly in resting after a workout and I do
it for around 10 minutes everyday, and longer if I have the time.
