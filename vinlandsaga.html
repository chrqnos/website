Vinland Saga

Thorfinn is son to one of the Viking's greatest warriors,
but when his father is killed in battle by the mercenary leader Askeladd,
he swears his intent to have revenge. Thorfinn joins Askeladd's
group in order to challenge him to a duel, and ends up caught in the
middle of a war for the crown of England.


Personal Opinion:
This story and main character is personally my favourite.
Just like Miyamoto Musashi, the main character of this manga,
Thorfinn, is also based off of a real historical figure.
Thorfinn's father is a very respectful man
and has taught Thorfinn to be just like him,
however after his death, Thorfinn becomes a
horrible person while fighting as a mercenary under his father's
killer. This story is really amazing seeing Thorfinn's devlopement.
The background of this site itself is an amazing panel.
